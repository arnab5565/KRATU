But we are sure that it will help many. And we are also trying to improve and add more things.

Kartu

Kratu is an idea, an idea to think and choose different. Idea to choose convinience. That is why we created Kratu editor and Kratu docs. Kratu editor is for new developers who are not so used to heavy IDEs. It is created so that they may learn the basics before shifting to any IDE. Kratu is just like notepad but much better. We are also trying to make Kratu editor for mobile, currently it is under development. We know the world is constantly evolving and the use of smartphones are increasing. But writing program codes in a smartphone can be a tidious job, so we are creating Kratu editor for smartphone to tackel this problem.
